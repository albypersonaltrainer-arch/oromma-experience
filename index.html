Your Curated Journey

Day 1 · October 21

Arrival & Settle In

Arrival in Munich, private airport pick-up, hotel check-in and a relaxed evening to settle into the experience.

Day 2 · October 22

Jewelry Fair & Bavarian Night

Early access to the jewelry fair, guided introduction to the key areas, supplier insights, and a traditional Bavarian dinner.

Day 3 · October 23

Fair Exploration & Dinner

Continued discovery and sourcing at the fair at your own pace, followed by dinner in a carefully chosen Munich setting.

Departure

Final breakfast, hotel pick-up and transfer to Munich Airport — a calm and well-arranged departure.
